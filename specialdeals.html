Get credit for trading in your current device. Get credit for trading in your current device.

The Apple Renew Program offers you an environmentally responsible way to part with your old devices. Whether it’s an iPhone, iPad, Mac, or even some non-Apple devices, working or non-working, it doesn’t matter. We’ll take it and determine if it qualifies for reuse. If it does, that means your device has monetary value and can be traded in for credit.1 You can trade in at apple.com or any Apple Store. If your device doesn’t qualify for reuse, we’ll recycle it responsibly at no cost to you.2
